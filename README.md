# 📊 Job Market Analysis Using Pandas & Matplotlib

## 📌 Project Overview

This project explores and analyzes a real-world job postings dataset to identify hiring trends, popular job categories, active recruiting companies, job locations, and in-demand skills.

The analysis was performed using **Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib** to gain insights into the current job market and visualize key patterns in the dataset.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze the distribution of job opportunities across industries.
* Identify the locations with the highest number of job postings.
* Determine the companies with the most job openings.
* Explore the most common job titles.
* Analyze hiring trends over time.
* Identify the most demanded technical skills.
* Visualize findings using charts and graphs.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset

Dataset Source:

https://huggingface.co/datasets/azrai99/job-dataset

The dataset contains thousands of job postings with information such as:

* Job Title
* Company Name
* Location
* Job Category
* Job Subcategory
* Job Type
* Job Description
* Listing Date

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed

### 1. Top Job Categories

Identified the industries with the highest number of job openings.

### 2. Top Hiring Locations

Analyzed which locations offer the most employment opportunities.

### 3. Job Type Distribution

Studied the proportion of Full-Time, Contract, Part-Time, and Casual jobs.

### 4. Top Hiring Companies

Determined the companies posting the highest number of vacancies.

### 5. Most Common Job Titles

Identified the roles that appear most frequently in the job market.

### 6. Hiring Trends Over Time

Examined how job postings change over different time periods.

### 7. Most Demanded Technical Skills

Analyzed job descriptions to identify frequently requested skills such as:

* Python
* SQL
* Java
* JavaScript
* React
* Node.js
* AWS

### 8. Top Job Subcategories

Explored specialized job domains with high demand.

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Certain industries dominate the hiring market.
* A few locations contribute a large share of job opportunities.
* Full-time positions represent the majority of job listings.
* Several companies consistently recruit more candidates than others.
* Technical skills remain highly sought after across multiple industries.
* Hiring activity varies over time and may reflect market trends.
